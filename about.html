ABOUT US

When Kiki’s Cafe began roasting coffee in 2005, our intent was to provide top-quality coffees to the community. But once people began coming to us for coffee in masses, we knew it was time to expand. A bigger and brighter storefront allowed us to not only make great coffeefor our customers, but also teach them how to brew it best themselves. Now, we can share our passion with more people.

Cappuccino or just plain coffee? Frozen or cold brew? Whether you’re a coffee connoisseur, new to the coffee-drinking experience or somewhere in between, we want everyone to find something they love. Expertly crafted coffee doesn’t have to feel exclusive.

Our staff is eager to make recommendations and further your coffee. Stop in to sample our coffee of the day or our featured flavor, just don’tforget to scratch Kiki behind the ears before you go on your way.
